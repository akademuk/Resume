About Me

With over three years of professional experience in development, I specialize in creating clear, semantic, and responsive markup using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. My attention to pixel-perfect implementation and accessibility (WCAG standards) ensures that the interfaces I create not only reflect the design concept but also work seamlessly across different devices and browsers.

Throughout my career, I have successfully implemented over 150 commercial projects of varying complexity, from landing pages to large-scale web applications. In addition to development, I regularly review code to maintain high standards of code quality and provide estimates of design implementation time to help clients and teams plan projects more effectively.

With solid experience working with Git, preprocessors (Sass/LESS), and modern build tools, I successfully collaborate with designers and backend developers to create high-quality, maintainable code that drives business results.
